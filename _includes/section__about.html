ML

The Motor Lab was created in 2023 by the MIT Electric Vehicle Team.

The MIT Motor Lab (ML) is an open-source resource center for motor design, motor control, motor construction, and motor testing particularly related to robotics and electric vehicles. It combines multiple different sources of information into a central knowledge-based provided to the public free-of-charge.

The content on this website is compiled from multiple sources and contributors, if you'd like to contribute to motors.mit.edu, feel free to reach out to us!
